# UR Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** May 13, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 13, 2026

## 1. Our Community Values

UR is built on the principle that **creators deserve a space to share their authentic selves—whether that's art, life, or mental health journeys.** We believe in fostering a community that is:

- **Clean and Honest:** Free from deception, fraud, and harmful content
- **Community-Based:** Respectful, inclusive, and supportive of all creators and followers
- **Safe and Welcoming:** Where everyone feels secure and valued

These Community Guidelines ensure that UR remains a positive space for all users. By using UR, you agree to follow these guidelines.

## 2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

### 2.1 Violence and Harm

Users must not:

- Threaten, encourage, or promote violence against any person or group
- Share content depicting graphic violence, gore, or self-harm
- Encourage suicide, self-injury, or eating disorders
- Engage in bullying, harassment, or coordinated attacks
- Promote dangerous challenges or activities that could cause harm

**Exception:** Educational content about mental health, recovery, and harm prevention is permitted.

### 2.2 Hate Speech and Discrimination

Users must not:

- Use slurs, hateful language, or dehumanizing speech targeting any group
- Promote discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Incite hatred or violence against any individual or group
- Share conspiracy theories that target specific groups

**Exception:** Discussing discrimination, racism, or historical injustices for educational purposes is permitted.

### 2.3 Sexual and Exploitative Content

Users must not:

- Share non-consensual intimate images or deepfakes
- Engage in child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child exploitation
- Solicit sexual services or engage in sex trafficking
- Share graphic sexual content (with limited exceptions for educational content)
- Engage in sexual harassment or coercion

**Exception:** Consensual adult content creators may share content marked as adult-only, subject to platform policies.

### 2.4 Illegal Activities

Users must not:

- Promote, facilitate, or engage in illegal activities
- Share instructions for creating weapons, drugs, or explosives
- Engage in fraud, scams, or financial crimes
- Facilitate human trafficking or modern slavery
- Distribute counterfeit goods or intellectual property
- Engage in money laundering or terrorist financing

### 2.5 Misinformation and Harmful Falsehoods

Users must not:

- Spread deliberate misinformation about health, safety, or elections
- Share false information designed to cause panic or harm
- Impersonate others or create fake accounts to deceive users
- Engage in coordinated inauthentic behavior or manipulation

**Exception:** Satire, parody, and opinion are permitted when clearly labeled.

### 2.6 Spam and Manipulation

Users must not:

- Post repetitive, unsolicited messages or advertisements
- Engage in phishing, social engineering, or account hacking
- Artificially inflate engagement through bots or fake accounts
- Manipulate algorithms through coordinated behavior
- Share malware, viruses, or malicious links
- Engage in pyramid schemes or multi-level marketing

### 2.7 Intellectual Property Violations

Users must not:

- Upload content they do not have the right to share
- Infringe on copyrights, trademarks, or patents
- Plagiarize or pass off others' work as their own
- Violate music licensing or royalty requirements

**Exception:** Fair use for commentary, criticism, or education may be permitted.

### 2.8 Privacy Violations

Users must not:

- Share others' personal information without consent (doxing)
- Record or share private conversations without consent
- Violate others' privacy or confidentiality
- Stalk, harass, or monitor others without consent

## 3. Creator Responsibilities

### 3.1 Accurate Representation

Creators agree to:

- Accurately describe the content and services they provide
- Use honest pricing and not mislead followers about value
- Disclose sponsored content, affiliate relationships, and conflicts of interest
- Not engage in catfishing or impersonation

### 3.2 Content Delivery

Creators agree to:

- Deliver content and services as promised to followers
- Respond to followers in a timely and professional manner
- Honor refund requests as per platform policy
- Not exploit or manipulate followers

### 3.3 Respectful Engagement

Creators agree to:

- Treat followers with respect and professionalism
- Not engage in harassment, discrimination, or abuse
- Establish and enforce boundaries with followers
- Report and block users who violate guidelines

## 4. Follower Responsibilities

### 4.1 Respectful Behavior

Followers agree to:

- Treat creators with respect and professionalism
- Not engage in harassment, threats, or abusive messages
- Respect creators' boundaries and content policies
- Not attempt to circumvent payment systems or access content without payment

### 4.2 Intellectual Property Respect

Followers agree to:

- Not share, reproduce, or distribute creators' content without permission
- Respect creators' copyright and intellectual property rights
- Not record or screenshot private conversations without consent

## 5. Content Moderation and Enforcement

### 5.1 Reporting Violations

Users can report content or behavior that violates these guidelines by:

- Using the report button on any post, message, or profile
- Contacting our support team at support@urapp.com
- Providing specific details about the violation

All reports are reviewed by our moderation team and treated confidentially.

### 5.2 Moderation Actions

Depending on the severity and nature of violations, UR may take the following actions:

- **Warning:** Notification of guideline violation with opportunity to comply
- **Content Removal:** Deletion of violating content
- **Account Suspension:** Temporary restriction of account access (24 hours to 30 days)
- **Permanent Ban:** Permanent termination of account and access to the Platform

### 5.3 Repeat Violations

Users who repeatedly violate these guidelines may face escalated enforcement, including permanent account termination.

### 5.4 Severe Violations

Severe violations (such as CSAM, human trafficking, or violent threats) may result in immediate permanent ban and reporting to law enforcement.

## 6. Appeal Process

### 6.1 Appealing Moderation Decisions

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, you may appeal by:

- Emailing appeals@urapp.com within 30 days of the action
- Providing specific details about why you believe the decision was incorrect
- Submitting any evidence supporting your appeal

### 6.2 Appeal Review

Our appeals team will review your submission and respond within 7-10 business days. Decisions made on appeal are final.

### 6.3 Expedited Review

Users may request expedited review if the moderation action significantly impacts their ability to use the Platform.

## 7. Special Considerations

### 7.1 Mental Health Content

We recognize that mental health discussions are central to UR's mission. Content about mental health, recovery, and personal struggles is encouraged and protected. However, content that glorifies self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders is prohibited.

### 7.2 Sensitive Topics

Creators may discuss sensitive topics (trauma, abuse, loss, etc.) as long as content is handled respectfully and includes appropriate warnings or content labels.

### 7.3 Educational Content

Educational content about sensitive topics (sex education, drug awareness, etc.) is permitted when presented factually and responsibly.

## 8. Accessibility and Inclusivity

UR is committed to being accessible and inclusive for all users. We encourage:

- Using captions and transcripts for audio and video content
- Using alt text for images
- Using clear, simple language
- Being mindful of different abilities and backgrounds

## 9. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

UR cooperates with law enforcement and may disclose user information when required by law or when we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent illegal activity or protect public safety.

## 10. Changes to Guidelines

UR reserves the right to modify these Community Guidelines at any time. Changes become effective upon posting. We encourage users to review these guidelines periodically.
