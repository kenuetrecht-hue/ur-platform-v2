# UR Loyalty Points & Sticker System Rules

## Overview

The UR platform features a comprehensive loyalty points and sticker (stars) system designed to reward user engagement, incentivize purchases, and create meaningful interactions within the community.

---

## Loyalty Points System

### What Are Loyalty Points?

Loyalty points are a virtual currency earned through various activities on the UR platform. Users can accumulate points and spend them on premium features, sticker packs, and exclusive content. Points also contribute to tier progression.

### How to Earn Loyalty Points

| Activity | Base Points | Tier Multiplier | Details |
|----------|------------|-----------------|---------|
| **Daily Login** | 5 | Bronze: 1.0x, Silver: 1.2x, Gold: 1.5x, Platinum: 2.0x | One per day, resets at midnight UTC |
| **Follow Creator** | 10-50 | Bronze: 1.0x, Silver: 1.1x, Gold: 1.2x, Platinum: 1.5x | Varies by creator tier (Bronze: 10, Silver: 20, Gold: 35, Platinum: 50) |
| **Tip Creator** | 1 per $1 | Bronze: 1.0x, Silver: 1.1x, Gold: 1.2x, Platinum: 1.5x | 1 point earned for every $1 tipped |
| **Sticker Purchase** | 1 per $1 | Bronze: 1.0x, Silver: 1.15x, Gold: 1.25x, Platinum: 1.5x | Points equal to sticker pack price (e.g., $10 pack = 10 points) |
| **Successful Referral** | 50 | — | Friend must complete signup and first purchase |
| **Creator Reward** | 5-100 | — | Custom rewards set by creators for engagement |
| **7-Day Login Streak** | 5 | — | Extra bonus awarded on day 7; resets if streak breaks |
| **First Purchase Bonus** | 25 | — | One-time bonus on first sticker purchase |

### Monthly Points Cap

To prevent abuse and maintain balance, there is a monthly limit on points earned:

- **Bronze/Silver/Gold**: 500 points per month
- **Platinum**: 750 points per month
- **Bonus points** (referrals, streaks, collection completion) do not count toward the cap
- Cap resets on the 1st of each month at midnight UTC

### Points Expiration

- Points expire after **12 months of inactivity**
- Users receive a warning when points are expiring within 30 days
- Logging in or earning new points resets the expiration timer

Stickers are collectible digital assets that users can purchase, use in communications, and trade with other users. Each sticker purchase includes both stickers and loyalty points.

### What Are Stars?

Stars represent the cumulative value of sticker purchases and are used to unlock exclusive features and badges:

- **1-3 Stars**: Access to basic sticker reactions and chat usage
- **5-7 Stars**: Unlock sticker trading with other users
- **10+ Stars**: VIP sticker gallery, monthly leaderboard eligibility
- **15+ Stars**: Exclusive sticker designs, priority creator support

### How to Use Stickers

#### In Chat Messages
- Use **1-5 stickers** per message to enhance communication
- Each sticker used earns **1 loyalty point**
- Stickers appear inline with your message for visual impact

#### Post Reactions
- React to posts with stickers instead of traditional likes
- Each reaction earns **2 loyalty points**
- Creators see sticker reactions as engagement metrics

#### Creator Appreciation
- Gift sticker packs directly to creators
- Costs **5-20 stickers** depending on pack size
- Earns **5-20 loyalty points** for the gifter
- Creators receive 100% of sticker value as earnings

#### Sticker Trading
- Trade duplicate stickers with other users
- Both parties earn **3 loyalty points** per trade
- Requires at least 5 stars to unlock trading
- Trades are peer-to-peer with built-in dispute resolution

#### Monthly Starter Pack
- All users receive **20 free stickers** on the 1st of each month
- Resets automatically at midnight UTC
- No purchase required—loyalty reward for active users

Your tier is determined by your total loyalty points:

|------|-----------------|-------------|------------------|-------------|-------|
| **Bronze** | 0-99 | 5 pts | 1.0x | 500 | Standard support, Welcome bonus |
| **Silver** | 100-499 | 10 pts | 1.2x | 500 | 5% tip discount, Priority support, Silver badge, 10% sticker discount |
| **Gold** | 500-999 | 15 pts | 1.5x | 500 | 10% tip discount, Free monthly chat, Gold badge, Early access, 15% sticker discount |
| **Platinum** | 1000+ | 25 pts | 2.0x | 750 | 15% tip discount, Free monthly chat, Platinum badge, VIP support, Exclusive contests, 25% sticker discount |

### Tier Benefits Breakdown

**Tier Multipliers**: Higher tiers earn more points from the same activities (e.g., a Platinum user earns 2x points for daily login vs. Bronze user).

**Point Discounts**: When redeeming points for premium features, higher tiers get discounts:
- Sticker pack redemption: Bronze 0%, Silver 10%, Gold 15%, Platinum 25%
- Premium features: Platinum members get 15% discount

**Exclusive Perks**: Each tier unlocks unique features and content.

## Rules & Restrictions

### Point Earning Restrictions

1. **One daily login bonus per 24-hour period** (resets at midnight UTC)
2. **Monthly point cap** prevents earning more than 500-750 points per month
3. **Bonus points** (referrals, streaks, collection completion) do not count toward the cap
4. **Tier multipliers** apply to eligible activities but cannot exceed monthly cap
5. **First purchase bonus** is awarded only once per account

### Sticker Usage Restrictions

1. **Maximum 5 stickers per chat message**
2. **Sticker trades** require at least 5 stars
3. **Creator gifts** must be 5-100 stickers
4. **Monthly starter pack** resets on the 1st of each month
5. **Duplicate stickers** can be traded but not sold

### Point Expiration & Maintenance

1. **Points expire after 12 months of inactivity**
2. **Warning notification** sent 30 days before expiration
3. **Logging in or earning points** resets the expiration timer
4. **Expired points** are permanently removed and cannot be recovered
5. **Account deletion** results in loss of all points and stickers

## FAQ

**Q: Can I transfer points to another user?**
A: No, points are non-transferable and account-specific.

**Q: What happens to my points if I delete my account?**
A: All points and stickers are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

**Q: Can I earn points offline?**
A: No, points are only earned through active engagement on the platform.

**Q: How do I check my points expiration date?**
A: Visit your loyalty dashboard to see expiration dates and receive warnings.

**Q: Can creators set custom point rewards?**
A: Yes, creators can define custom rewards (5-100 points) for specific engagement actions.

**Q: What's the difference between points and stars?**
A: Points are earned through activities and used for redemptions. Stars represent cumulative sticker purchase value and unlock exclusive features.

**Q: Can I buy points directly?**
A: No, points must be earned through platform activities. However, you can purchase sticker packs, which include bonus points.

**Q: How are sticker trades taxed?**
A: Both parties earn 3 points per trade. There is no tax or fee deducted.

## Implementation Notes

### Service Files

- **loyalty-rules.ts**: Core loyalty system rules and calculations
- **loyalty-rules-service.ts**: Service wrapper for accessing loyalty rules
- **sticker-usage.ts**: Sticker usage tracking and mechanics

### Key Functions

- `calculatePointsEarned()`: Calculate points based on source and tier
- `calculatePointUsageCost()`: Calculate redemption cost with tier discount
- `recordChatUsage()`: Track sticker usage in messages
- `recordReactionUsage()`: Track sticker reactions
- `recordCreatorGift()`: Track sticker gifts to creators
- `initiateTrade()`: Start sticker trade between users
- `getMonthlyLeaderboard()`: Retrieve current month's leaderboard
- `getPointsExpiringWarning()`: Check if points are expiring soon
